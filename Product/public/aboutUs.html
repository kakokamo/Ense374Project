About Us:

Our Story:
During the month of September, 2024, a group of boys chose to be in a required group project for their Software Engineering Management class. The main goal for the class project was to choose an idea that teams will design and develop a web-based software application of their choice by utilizing project/software engineering management principles and Model-View-Controller (MVC) architecture/pattern. Each team were also given a bunch of design constraints which they had to choose 4/6 of the constraints to follow. Following the four design constraints, we decided to implement a web application that allows people in Saskatchewan to choose a hiking trail that they would like to do(This was the easiest idea out of the rest we came up with). Then we had to present our project to our class professor(amazing prof) and to our fellow classmates as well. Since then, we having been updating our site to give users the best experience they can have in choosing their trail destination to hike in.
